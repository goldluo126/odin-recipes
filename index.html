Indes

Indes is a traditional dish from the south of France. It is a type of stew made with meat, vegetables, and a variety of spices. The dish is typically served with rice or bread. Indes is a popular dish in France and is often enjoyed during the winter months.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of beef broth
  • 1 tsp of paprika
  • 1 tsp of cumin
  • 1 tsp of coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, brown the beef over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  3. Add the bell pepper, zucchini, tomatoes, beef broth, and spices.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot with rice or bread.
